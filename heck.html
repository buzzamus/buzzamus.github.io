Heck

Defend Heck from the forces of heaven

You play as the last demon guarding the gates of heck from the evil angelic army from above.

Use your fireball to destroy the angels and see if you can defeat all 666 of heaven's soldiers.

Move carefully, but be quick: The angels keep spawning so, if you wait too long their forces will overrun you.
